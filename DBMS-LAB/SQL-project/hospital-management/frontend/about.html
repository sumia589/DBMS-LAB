Who We Are

Dedicated to Your Wellbeing

MediCare General Hospital has been a cornerstone of healthcare excellence for over four decades. We combine cutting-edge medical technology with a compassionate, patient-first approach to deliver outstanding care across all specialties.

From routine checkups to complex surgeries, our team of 150+ specialist doctors and dedicated staff work around the clock to ensure every patient receives the best possible treatment in a safe, comfortable environment.

Our Journey

History & Milestones

1985 — Foundation

MediCare Hospital opens its doors with 50 beds and a vision for accessible healthcare.

1998 — Expansion

Hospital expands to 200 beds and introduces specialized cardiology and neurology departments.

2010 — Modernization

State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and digital health records introduced.

2024 — Today

Now serving 100,000+ patients annually with 500 beds and 150+ specialist doctors.
